What is Glaucoma?

Glaucoma is a group of eye diseases that damage the optic nerve and can lead to vision loss and blindness. It often has no symptoms in its early stages.

Glaucoma Facts

  • Elevated intraocular pressure is a major risk factor for glaucoma.
  • Regular eye exams are essential for early detection and treatment.
  • Glaucoma can affect people of all ages, but the risk increases with age.
